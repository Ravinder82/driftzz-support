Subscription & Billing Help

  • Premium Access: Insights and Immersive Soundscapes are premium features. Dashboard, Breathing timer, and Journey remain free.
  • Management: Manage or cancel subscriptions in iPhone Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions.
  • Restore: Use the "Restore Purchases" button on the Subscription screen to sync your active plan.
  • Billing: All payments and refunds are handled processed exclusively by Apple. For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Driftzz free to use?

Yes. The core breathing journey, daily dashboard, and basic breathing session are free. Access to advanced Insights and the full Soundscapes library requires a subscription.

The app is not working correctly

Ensure you are on the latest version from the App Store. If issues persist, please email us with your device model and iOS version.
