Subscription & Billing Help

  • Access Model: Driftzz gives each signed-in user 7 days of full access on the device where the app is used. After that access period ends, a yearly Driftzz Pro subscription is required to continue premium access.
  • Management: Manage or cancel subscriptions in iPhone Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions, or from the app’s Manage Subscription button.
  • Restore: Use the "Restore" option on the Subscription screen to sync your active yearly plan.
  • Billing: Driftzz Pro is a yearly auto-renewing subscription billed through Apple. The yearly plan renews at $19.99/year unless canceled at least 24 hours before the current billing period ends.
  • Refunds: Billing and refunds are handled directly by Apple. For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
