Subscription & Billing Help

  • Insights is the premium feature. Dashboard, Breathing, and Journey remain free.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in iPhone Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases from the app's Subscription screen using "Restore".
  • Billing and refunds are handled by Apple. For refund requests, use Apple's reportaproblem portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Driftzz free to use?

Yes. Dashboard, Breathing, and Journey are free. Insights requires an active subscription.

The app is not working correctly

Please update to the latest version. If the issue continues, email us with device model, iOS version, and screenshots.

How do I report a bug?

Email us steps to reproduce, expected behavior, and what actually happened.
